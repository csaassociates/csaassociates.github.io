About Us

CSA & Associates is engaged in providing corporate, regulatory and compliance advisory services to startups, growing businesses and listed entities. The firm assists clients in understanding and complying with requirements under corporate laws, SEBI regulations and FEMA provisions.

The scope of services includes incorporation, regulatory filings, corporate restructuring, listing compliances, secretarial audit and ongoing advisory support. The firm aims to provide structured assistance in handling compliance requirements in an efficient and systematic manner.

CSA & Associates follows a practical approach towards compliance management with focus on timely execution, proper documentation and adherence to applicable laws.
