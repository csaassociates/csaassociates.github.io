About Us

CSA & Associates is engaged in providing corporate, regulatory and compliance advisory services to startups, growing businesses and listed entities. The firm focuses on assisting clients in understanding and complying with requirements under corporate laws, SEBI regulations and FEMA provisions.

Our services cover a wide range of areas including incorporation, regulatory filings, corporate restructuring, listing compliance and ongoing advisory support. The objective is to provide structured and practical solutions aligned with applicable legal and regulatory frameworks.

The firm follows a systematic approach towards compliance management, ensuring timely execution, proper documentation and adherence to applicable laws. We aim to support businesses in managing regulatory requirements efficiently while focusing on their core operations.
